## Brand principles

### Institutional value before technology fashion

We select technology according to the problem, the operating environment and the long-term interests of the client.

### Independence by design

We favour open standards, portable architectures and open-source technologies that reduce unnecessary vendor dependence.

### Systems that survive launch day

A successful system must remain supportable, secure and useful after implementation. We therefore consider hosting, maintenance, user adoption, data ownership and operational continuity from the beginning.

### Local understanding, enterprise discipline

We understand Botswana’s institutional environment while applying sound engineering, governance and delivery practices.

Use language that is direct, intelligent and confident. Avoid inflated marketing claims, vague digital-transformation jargon and descriptions that make Skymouse sound like a generic creative agency.
