# Solutions

## Hero

### Headline

Technology designed around the way institutions actually work.

### Introduction

Skymouse helps organisations replace fragmented processes, manual controls and disconnected tools with digital systems that improve visibility, accountability and execution.

## Governance technology

Boards, committees and executive teams generate decisions, obligations and institutional records that must be prepared, controlled, tracked and preserved. We design platforms that turn governance from a collection of documents and email threads into a structured operating system.

### Capabilities

- Board and committee workspaces
- Meeting scheduling and preparation
- Agendas and board-pack distribution
- Controlled document access
- Resolutions and decision registers
- Action tracking and accountability
- Voting and approvals
- Conflict-of-interest declarations
- Governance dashboards and audit trails

## Enterprise application development

We build purpose-designed software for organisations whose processes cannot be solved adequately by generic off-the-shelf tools.

### Capabilities

- Workflow and case-management systems
- Operational dashboards
- Revenue and billing support systems
- Performance-monitoring platforms
- Reporting and management information systems
- Data migration and integration
- Internal portals and self-service platforms
- Legacy-system modernisation

## Public-sector digital systems

Public institutions operate under specific governance, reporting, procurement, accountability and service-delivery requirements. We design systems with those realities in mind.

### Capabilities

- Institutional performance platforms
- Public-service portals
- Regulatory and compliance workflows
- Programme and project monitoring
- Municipal and revenue-process support
- Digital registers and records
- Executive and statutory reporting

## Websites and digital platforms

A serious institutional website is part publishing platform, part service channel and part public record. We create modern websites and portals that are credible, accessible, maintainable and easy for authorised teams to manage.

### Capabilities

- Corporate and institutional websites
- Property and portfolio platforms
- Research and knowledge portals
- Application and opportunity portals
- Content architecture and migration
- Headless content-management systems
- Accessibility and performance optimisation
- Hosting, maintenance and analytics

## Managed open-source infrastructure

Open-source platforms can give organisations powerful capability and greater independence, but they still require responsible deployment and management. Skymouse can configure, host, integrate and support selected platforms as managed services.

### Capabilities

- Private-cloud and collaboration platforms
- Digital-signature and approval systems
- Document and knowledge management
- Secure VPS and container hosting
- Backups and disaster-recovery planning
- Monitoring, patching and maintenance
- Reverse proxy, SSL and domain configuration
- Integration with existing business systems

## Data, reporting and integration

Institutions often have valuable data trapped across spreadsheets, legacy databases and departmental applications. We help make that data usable without pretending every existing system must be discarded.

### Capabilities

- Database analysis and remediation
- SQL development and optimisation
- API design and systems integration
- Data migration and reconciliation
- Management reports and dashboards
- Legacy accounting and operational-system integration

## Advisory and solution design

Some organisations need clarity before they need code. We help define the problem, target operating model, system requirements, architecture, implementation approach and long-term ownership model.

### Capabilities

- Digital strategy and technology roadmaps
- Business and system requirements
- Solution architecture

- Implementation planning
